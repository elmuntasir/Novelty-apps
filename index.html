FAQ

Good to know before you install.

Is Novelty free?

Yes — your first book project is free with no time limit and unlimited words. Unlocking unlimited book projects is a one-time $35 payment, handled securely inside the app via Polar.sh.

Where is my writing stored?

Locally, on your machine. Novelty doesn't require an account or cloud sync to write and export — your manuscript stays yours.

How does the $35 upgrade work?

Download and use Novelty for free on one book. When you're ready for more, unlock unlimited projects from inside the app — it's a single one-time payment, not a subscription.

What export formats does it support?

Professional, KDP-ready PDF and EPUB exports with custom front matter and a full catalog of chapter styles you can apply per project.

Which platforms are supported?

Novelty runs on macOS (Apple Silicon), Windows (x64 and ARM64), and Linux (AppImage or .deb) — download links for all of them are above.
